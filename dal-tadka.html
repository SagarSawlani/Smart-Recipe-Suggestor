Dal Tadka

Ingredients

  • 1 cup yellow lentils (toor dal)
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 2 teaspoons cumin seeds
  • 2 tablespoons ghee or oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Chopped coriander for garnish

Instructions

  1. Wash and soak lentils for 30 minutes. Drain.
  2. In a pot, boil lentils with water and turmeric until soft.
  3. In another pan, heat ghee. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  4. Add chopped onions and saut√© until golden.
  5. Add tomatoes and cook until soft. Season with salt.
  6. Mix the cooked lentils into the onion-tomato mixture.
  7. Simmer for 5 minutes. Garnish with coriander and serve hot.
