Pizza

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 packet yeast
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Your choice of toppings (pepperoni, bell peppers, onions, etc.)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for 5 minutes.
  2. Add flour, salt, and olive oil to the yeast mixture. Knead until smooth.
  3. Cover and let the dough rise for 1 hour in a warm place.
  4. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).
  5. Roll out the dough and place it on a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  6. Spread tomato sauce over the dough, then add cheese and toppings.
  7. Bake for 15-20 minutes until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
  8. Let cool for a few minutes, slice, and enjoy!
