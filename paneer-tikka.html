Paneer Tikka

Ingredients

  • 250 grams paneer, cut into cubes
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons tandoori masala
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 bell pepper, cut into chunks
  • 1 onion, cut into chunks
  • Skewers

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix yogurt, tandoori masala, ginger-garlic paste, and lemon juice.
  2. Add paneer, bell pepper, and onion to the marinade. Let it sit for 1 hour.
  3. Preheat the grill or oven to 200°C (392°F).
  4. Skewer the marinated paneer and vegetables alternately.
  5. Grill for 15-20 minutes, turning occasionally until charred.
  6. Serve hot with mint chutney.
